We further show that there can be no real  relational structure of scale type (m, m + 1), m>,2. These results parallel those given in Narens (1981b) for real relational  structures of scale types (2, 2) and (m, m), m > 2, repectively. Indeed, the sequence of lemmas used here is taken almost  directly from Narens (1981b), simply replacing 2point homogeneity by 1-point homogeneity whenever possible. Lemmas 1-6 are  proven in Narens (1981b) without using 2-point homogeneity; therefore they may be used for the scale type (1, 2) case without  change of argument. The proofs for many of the subsequent lemmas may also be taken from that source as their arguments does  not directly involve 2-point homogeneity and all supporting lemmas used in their proof have been reproven here without  assuming 2-point homogeneity. This approach has made necessary some redundance in the proof (as in Lemma 17), but it serves to  show how very similar the two cases are. As an aid in cross-reference, for each lemma, the number of the corresponding lemma  in Narens (1981b) is given in brackets.
