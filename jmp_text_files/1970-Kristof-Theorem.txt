The main purpose of this paper consists in establishing attainable upper and lower limits for the trace of certain products of  real matrices. These products are of the form XII'IXZI~ --- Xnr,, with orthogonal matrices Xi and diagonal matrices F,. In  Sec. I, the range of the trace of such products under independent and unrestricted variation of Xi is formulated as a theorem.  The proof makes use of two lemmas which are established first.  This theorem may deserve interest in its own right. In addition, it is a useful tool in deriving results of psychometric  interest when the trace of matrices is involved. In Sec. II, several examples of such applications are given. Invocation of  the theorem there seems to shorten and simplify the derivations.
