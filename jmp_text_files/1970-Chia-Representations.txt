CHIA~  Monash University, Victoria, Australia  Received November 1, 1968  It is shown that the N-element pattern model with r alternative responses is characterised by a r x r stochastic matrix P. A  spectral representation of the transition probability matrix is possible if r is diagonalisable. In particular, we obtain  explicit expressions for the nth step transition probabilities of the general N-element pattern model with two responses and  also of the N-element pattern model with r responses in the simple noncontingent case. It is further shown that the stationary  distribution of the general model can be obtained very easily and is a multinomial distribution whose values are independent  of the conditioning parameter c.
