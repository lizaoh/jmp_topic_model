TAYLOR  Acadia University, Wolfville, Nova Scotia, Canada  Theorems for relational systems with a Thomsen or Reidemeister cancellation condition are presented together with an  application of one of the theorems. The application provides a weaker form of the Luce-Tukey solvability axiom for conjoint  measurement.  INTRODUCTION  This note is concerned with relational systems which have cancellation conditions corresponding to the Reidemeister or Thomsen  closure conditions of net theory. The theorems presented are not proven directly but it is shown how they may, be reduced to  known results obtained for nets.
