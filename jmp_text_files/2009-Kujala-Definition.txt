We have developed a radical generalization of the notion of well-behaved discrimination functions ( Definition 3 ). This generalization is achieved by applying a transfinite series of topological and transitive closures ( Definition 4  and  Theorem 2 ) to the well-behavedness relation ( Definitions 1 and 2 ) as introduced in  Kujala and Dzhafarov (2008) .
